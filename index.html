I am a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science, Princeton University, advised by Professor Jennifer Rexford. I work in the areas of networked systems, clouding computing and networking. I design and build scalable, efficient and secure networked systems to manage physical and virtual networks and optimize data delivery for large-scale data-intensive computing and mobile applications. I am supported by the Charlotte Elizabeth Procter Fellowship. I am also a Siebel Scholar, Class of 2016.

I am currently on the academic job market for a tenure-track assistant professor position.
