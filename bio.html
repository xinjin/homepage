Short Bio

Xin Jin is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University starting in Fall 2017. He is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of EECS at UC Berkeley. He has a broad research interest in computer networking and distributed systems.He received his BS degree in computer science and BA degree in economics from Peking University in 2011, and his MA and PhD degree in computer science from Princeton University in 2013 and 2016. He has actively collaborated with leading research institutes and cutting-edge startups, including Microsoft Research, AT&T Labs, Bell Labs, Barefoot Networks, and Rockley Photonics.
